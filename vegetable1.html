Distribution

Local Farms

We partner with local farms and suppliers who specialize in growing high-quality, fresh vegetables. These farms often utilize sustainable and organic farming practices, ensuring that the vegetables are not only delicious but also environmentally friendly.

Wholesale Markets

Our vegetables also come from reputable wholesale markets known for their wide selection and fresh produce. These markets have stringent quality control measures in place to ensure that only the finest vegetables make it to our supermarket shelves.

International Importers

To provide exotic and specialty vegetables, we collaborate with international importers who specialize in sourcing produce from around the world. These importers have extensive networks and expertise in global agriculture, allowing us to offer unique vegetables that are not commonly found locally.

In-House Greenhouses

In addition to external suppliers, we also operate in-house greenhouses where we grow a selection of vegetables year-round. Our greenhouses are equipped with state-of-the-art technology to control temperature, humidity, and light, creating optimal growing conditions for our produce.
